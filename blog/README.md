## What Village Map Joiner does

Village maps downloaded from the Tamil Nadu **Commissionerate of Survey and
Settlement** come as separate survey sheets — each a PDF with a black frame and a
white margin. The sheets are **adjacent, not overlapping**, so they can't be
auto-stitched. Village Map Joiner removes every bit of friction around aligning
them: it renders each PDF at high resolution, auto-crops the frame, makes the white
background transparent, snaps the seams together, and exports one clean PNG or a
print-ready A0–A4 PDF — all **in your browser**, with nothing uploaded.
