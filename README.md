## What is Village Map Joiner?

Village Map Joiner is a browser-based tool to stitch individual Tamil Nadu cadastral and FMB-style village survey sheets into one continuous, print-ready map. No software to install. No files uploaded to any server. Designed specifically for Tamil Nadu government survey sheets from the Survey & Settlement portal.

---

## Features

### Editor Tools
- **Smart Snapping** — edge-snap guides and automatic alignment to fit sheets together like jigsaw pieces
- **Auto Frame-Cropping** — removes borders automatically for clean joining
- **White-to-Transparent Seam Cleaning** — eliminates white lines between joined sheets
- **Nudge Controls** — fine-tune positioning for pixel-perfect alignment
- **Visual Preview** — see the full combined map before exporting

### Privacy
- **100% in-browser processing** — your files are never uploaded to a server
- Your land records stay on your device

### Export
- High-resolution PNG export
- Print-ready A0–A4 PDF export

---

## How It Works

### Self-Service (Free to use, ₹99 to export)
1. Download your individual survey sheets from the Tamil Nadu government portal
2. Upload them into the browser-based editor
3. Drag, snap, and arrange the sheets to form a continuous map
4. Preview the result
5. Export as PNG or PDF for ₹99 (one-time, per export)

### Done-for-You (Fully managed service)
1. Submit your land details — District, Taluk, Village, and Survey Number
2. The team sources, downloads, and stitches your maps
3. Receive your combined map via WhatsApp
